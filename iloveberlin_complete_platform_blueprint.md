# ILoveBerlin Complete Platform Blueprint

Website: iloveberlin.biz

Author: Product Blueprint Purpose: Full platform vision, scope,
structure, growth, and operational strategy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Executive Vision

ILoveBerlin is envisioned as the **digital lifestyle hub of Berlin**.

The platform combines:

-   City media
-   Event discovery
-   Restaurant discovery
-   Community storytelling
-   Local marketplace
-   Classified listings
-   Competitions
-   Video storytelling

The platform acts as the **digital gateway to Berlin life**.

Primary mission:

Help residents, visitors, and businesses discover everything happening
in Berlin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

The entire platform is built on seven major pillars.
